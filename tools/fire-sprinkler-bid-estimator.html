What it does

A first-pass estimate to price a job and write a proposal, built for sprinkler contractors and estimators:

  • Estimated head count from square footage and occupancy
  • Labor, material, and per-head rates you control
  • Simple or detailed markup
  • A proposal and cost-breakdown PDF

How to use it

  1. Enter the project square footage and occupancy.
  2. Adjust the labor, material, and markup rates to your shop.
  3. Read the estimate and export a proposal.

FAQ

How is the head count estimated?
It estimates coverage from the square footage and occupancy hazard to give a fast head count for pricing. Your final design determines the actual count.
Can I export a proposal?
Yes. The estimator produces a proposal and a cost breakdown PDF you can send to a customer.
