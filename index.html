Material submittals

Drop in cut sheets. Get a submittal package.

Pick your products or drop in PDFs and SprinkFlow assembles a formatted, bookmarked submittal — cover sheet, table of contents, and every datasheet in order. What used to be an afternoon of copy-paste is a couple of minutes.

  • Auto cover sheet, index, and bookmarks
  • Built-in manufacturer datasheet catalog
  • Save contractors and projects for reuse
AI-assisted import

The computer reads the cut sheet so you don't have to.

SprinkFlow uses Claude to read your manufacturer cut sheets and hydraulic calc packages — pulling model, K-factor, response type, temperature ratings, listings, and design criteria automatically, then filling the submittal for you. If anything's ever unavailable, it quietly falls back to the built-in parser.

  • Model, K-factor, listings & SIN map from cut sheets
  • Hazard, density, demand & supply from calcs
  • You review and tweak — it does the typing
Design tools

The reference tools you keep a tab open for — built in.

Storage commodity and hazard lookups, NFPA reference roadmaps, hydraulic remote-area organization, and more — all in the same place you build your submittals, so you're not bouncing between apps and PDFs.

  • Storage hazard & commodity classification
  • NFPA 13 reference roadmap
  • Hydraulic calc organization
