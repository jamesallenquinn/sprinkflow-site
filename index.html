Every tool a sprinkler designer reaches for.

Submittals are just the start. SprinkFlow packs the generators, calcs, lookups, and utilities you'd otherwise keep a dozen tabs and spreadsheets open for — all in one window.

Hydraulic Calculation Package

Your calcs, packaged as cleanly as your submittal.

Drop in your hydraulic calc PDFs and SprinkFlow organizes them into a clean, bookmarked package — edit the table of contents as needed, hit generate, done. The same cover-sheeted, indexed treatment as your material submittal, for your calcs.

  • Drag & drop calc PDFs
  • Editable table of contents
  • Cover sheet + bookmarks
Storage Analysis

Every NFPA storage option, side by side.

Pick your commodity and arrangement and SprinkFlow lays out every applicable NFPA 13 protection option at once — ESFR, CMSA, CMDA, in-rack — with densities and demands, across multiple code editions. Compare, choose, and export a clean analysis.

  • All NFPA 13 storage options compared
  • Multiple code years
  • Copy-ready analysis report
Water Supply Analysis

A flow test in, a clean supply curve out.

Enter your static, residual, and pitot readings and SprinkFlow plots the hydrant flow curve and builds a water-supply report you can drop straight into a submittal — with your demand point overlaid on the curve.

  • Hydrant flow-curve graph
  • Demand point overlay
  • Exportable report
Head Layout Generator

Even-spaced head layout, instantly.

Give it a room and a coverage area and it lays out an evenly-spaced head layout in a click — every dimension you need, ready to copy. Or export the layout to DXF and pull it straight into your design program.

  • Even-spacing layout in one click
  • Copy-ready dimensions
  • Export to DXF
Hanger Detail Generator

A sharp hanger detail without firing up CAD.

Build a clean fire-sprinkler hanger detail right in the browser and export it as a PNG or DXF. It's early — a focused set of options today, growing over time — but already a handy way to produce a crisp detail in seconds.

  • Draw a hanger detail in the browser
  • PNG + DXF export
  • Growing library of options
Bid Estimator

An estimator's best friend.

Enter the basics about a project and get an estimated head count and a standard estimating workflow — labor, materials, and markup — all in one place, with a proposal and breakdown PDF at the end.

  • Quick head-count estimate
  • Labor + material + markup
  • Proposal & breakdown PDF
Preliminary Calculation

Know your demand before you draw a thing.

Give it the basics of a project and it sizes up your demand up front. Behind the scenes it lays out a representative system and runs a real hydraulic pass — density/area, Hazen-Williams friction, elevation and backflow losses, K-factor start pressure — to estimate the flow and pressure you'll need, and flags whether your supply looks adequate.

  • A real hydraulic pass, not a rule of thumb
  • GPM / PSI demand estimate
  • Export straight to the Bid Estimator
Sprinkler Spacing Calculator

Check spacing — and the flow it demands.

Check your spacing in seconds, especially handy for those off-the-wall dimensions. It gives you the coverage area and the required GPM and PSI for that spacing — perfect for fine-tuning your calcs.

  • Coverage area from S & T dimensions
  • Required GPM & PSI
  • Great for off-the-wall layouts
Hangers / Bracing Spacing

Perfectly spaced bracing and hangers, every time.

Produce dimensions for evenly spaced sway bracing and hangers. It places lateral braces within 6 ft of the ends of the main and spaces the rest evenly in between — no more eyeballing it on the riser diagram.

  • Even hanger & brace spacing
  • Lateral braces within 6 ft of ends
  • Copy-ready dimensions
Feet / Inch Calculator

A calculator that speaks feet-and-inches.

Type an expression in feet-inches — like 12-6 + 8-3 × 2 — and get the answer right back in feet-inches. No more converting to decimals in your head and back again.

  • Feet-inch math with mixed operators
  • Answers in feet-inches
  • Built for the tape-measure world
NFPA Code Assist

Enter a section number and SprinkFlow maps it across NFPA 13 editions, plus a searchable roadmap of common references — so you spend less time flipping through the index.

  • Section converter across editions

  • Less time in the index
